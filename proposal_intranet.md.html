These include the PAN form, New Hire packets, Health insurance forms, etc. Estimated timeline for completion. Determine how to incorporate the new hire paperwork as part of an intranet business process, how to generate an employee file/profile from the new hire packet information, how to use the electronic signature with federal documents, etc. Estimated timeline for completion - 1/15/2015 *Business Processes* What benchmarks and deadlines do we need to have for learning business processes? - needs - solutions available *Task Prioritization with our Consultant* - What do we want the consultant to do for us? - How long will the work take? - How do we determine priority for each item? *Communication Department Needs/Layout* Speak with Christine about estimated benchmarks and deadlines for getting the Comm team fully set up in intranet. *Permissions Schema* Flesh out a basic permissions schema that can be used for departments in the stores. This may not be entirely portable to each store, but having a basic structure to copy and paste as a part of the intranet setup will prevent stores from having to set their permissions from scratch. This will also allow departmental support staff to prepare documents and information according to its intended audience. Estimated timeline for completion - 1/31/2015 *External Integration* Work with the ADP API to determine compatibility, export/import of employee information, our ability to push data from intranet to ADP (we are scheduled to migrate to ADP version 7 on 1/21/2015). Work with Active Directory to determine compatibility with intranet, and how that will function for employees in different positions/with different permissions. Determine what functionality we need from Google Drive and how to seamlessly integrate Google Drive document editing, forms, and spreadsheet data within the intranet. Estimated timeline for completion - 2/16/2015 Anything that falls outside the scope of these main areas can be added to a list of desired features to be incorporated at a later date.
